About Me

Hello world! I am Qing Ke (柯庆), a first, second, third-year Ph.D. student in Informatics (Complex Systems track) at CNetS, Indiana University Bloomington. I am broadly interested in (i) structural patterns of complex networks, (ii) science of science, and (iii) statistical learning. My advisor is Yong-Yeol Ahn. I am closely working with Alessandro Flammini and Filippo Radicchi.

Before I came to IUB, I got my B.E. (Control Technology and Instrument) and M.E. (Computer Science and Technology) from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (北京邮电大学) in 2009 and 2012, respectively (see pg. 23 of The Elements of Style).

Reach me through qke [AT] indiana [DOT] edu, or Rm 310, 919 E. 10th Street.
